Best movies of all time according to Samira(Me)

My top three movies of all time

Enola Holmes

TMy top favorite movie because of its clever mysteries, strong characters, and Enola's intelligence, independence, and determination. The story is exciting, inspiring, and full of unexpected twists.

Dune

A visually stunning sci-fi masterpiece with an incredible storyline, breathtaking world-building, and unforgettable characters. It keeps you invested from beginning to end.

Spiderman: No Way Home

An action-packed superhero movie filled with emotion, humor, and memorable moments. The story, character development, and thrilling action scenes make it one of the best Spider-Man films.
